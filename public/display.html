P.B.B Overnight Oats

2 hours

Ingredients
1/2 medium banana mashed,2 tbsp peanut butter, 1/4 cup of non-fat greek yogurt plain, 3/4 cup Almond milk, 1 tbsp honey, 1 cup rolled oats, 1 tsp cinnamon

Steps
1. Mash half of the banana in large bowl.
2.Add the rest of the wet ingredients.
3.Add in the dry ingredients and mix again.
4.Place you oats in the refridgerator covered for at least two hours or overnight.

Breakfast Cuisine: Other

Mac and Cheese

25 minutes

Ingredients
8 ounces macaroni, 1/8 cup butter, 1/8 cup flour, 1/4 tsp salt, black pepper, 1 cup milk, 1 cup of shedded cheese


Steps
1.Cook macaroni according to it's package's instructions.
2.Melt butter into a medium saucepan then stir in flour, salt, pepper.
3.Slowly add in milk. Keep stiring until bubbling.
4.Stir in cheese and then cook until melted.
