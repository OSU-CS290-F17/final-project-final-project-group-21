P.B.B Overnight Oats 1/2 medium banana mashed, 2 tbsp peanut butter, 1/4 cup of non-fat greek yogurt plain, 3/4 cup Almond milk, 1 tbsp honey, 1 tsp vanilla extract, 1 cup rolled oats, 1 tbsp chia seeds, 1 tsp cinnamon Cuisine: Other (at least 2 hours) Breakfast P.B.B. Overnight Oats
First mash half of the banana in large bowl then add the rest of the wet ingredients. Add in the dry ingredients and mix again. Place you oats in the refridgerator covered for at least two hours or overnight.
